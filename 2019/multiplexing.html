By allowing the parallel analysis of multiple biological samples against focused subsets of probes, multi-well substrates permit a number of versatile multiplex assay designs in diagnostic applications.

As a world leader in this technology, Schott’s NEXTERION® brand of multiplex substrates utilize a high performance proprietary formulation to create standard or highly customizable patterned wells with hydrophobic barriers which avoid cross-contamination.

Formats:

  • Slide size (75.6 x 25 x 1 mm³) with 16-well patterning
  • MTP size (110 x 74 x 1 mm³) with 96-well patterning
  • Also available in custom formats and designs.
