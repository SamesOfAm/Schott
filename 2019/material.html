You can decide which material suits best for your needs. The standard materials we are using are BOROFLOAT® 33 and substrates made of D263®. Both materials can be used for most of the applications in life science and diagnostic industry. If your special application requires a very special substrate material (e.g. fused silica), we would be pleased to discuss your requirements. The SCHOTT group produces several hundred different types of glass, which means that the range of glass thicknesses available is very wide, ranging from 30 µm up to 254 mm depending on the glass type.

Material properties:

  • high chemical resistance
  • excellent transmission
  • low fluorescence
  • exceptional flatness
  • tight geometric tolerances

The NEXTERION® uncoated substrates are available exclusively cleanroom cleaned (ISO class 5) and will be offered in standard and custom formats.
